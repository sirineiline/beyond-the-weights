MY VISION

ABOUT SIRINE

• MY JOURNEY

I am a student based in France, passionate about computer science and artificial intelligence. Born and raised here with Algerian roots, I speak French, Arabic, English, and am currently learning Korean. Alongside maintaining a consistent academic record in middle school, I actively participate in local student and youth governance, serving on my school's Board of Directors (CA) as well as the Youth Municipal Council (CMJ) and the Youth Departmental Council (CDJ). To share what I learn about technology and its impact on education, I created a blog and an English-language podcast named Beyond the Weights.

• MY PASSIONS

I am deeply interested in algorithmic ethics, data biases, and understanding how automated tools affect the way teenagers learn. This curiosity recently led me to conduct a survey among 97 of my classmates to write an analytical article on Medium exploring how generative AI shapes our learning environments. Beyond the technical side of computing, I am fascinated by international languages and cultures, which drives me to continuously improve my English and Korean skills to read global research.

• MY DREAMS & PROJECTS

My educational goal is to graduate high school and pursue a Bachelor’s degree in AI Systems at Yonsei University in South Korea. Following my undergraduate studies, I plan to apply for a Master’s degree and a PhD in the United States at institutions such as Stanford, MIT, UC Berkeley, or UCLA. My ultimate objective is to conduct advanced scientific research and work within leading AI organizations and technology companies like OpenAI and Google.
